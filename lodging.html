PLACES TO STAY

Hotels & Resorts

Taniti has one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Hostel

Looking to meet new people and have fun? Consider staying at the island hostel! There are four bunks to a room. Daily group activities are planned and hosted by the hostel staff.

Bed & Breakfasts

In search of a more intimate and cosy getaway? Come join us at a growing number of bed and breakfasts.
